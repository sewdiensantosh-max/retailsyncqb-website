Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Introduction

RetailSync QB values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using our application.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • QuickBooks Company ID
  • OAuth Access Tokens
  • Transaction Data synchronized with QuickBooks Online

How We Use Your Information

  • Synchronize retail sales into QuickBooks Online.
  • Authenticate securely using OAuth 2.0.
  • Improve application performance.
  • Provide customer support.

Data Security

RetailSync QB uses secure OAuth 2.0 authentication and encrypted communication with Intuit APIs. Your QuickBooks credentials are never stored.

Third-Party Services

RetailSync QB communicates with QuickBooks Online using the official Intuit APIs. We do not sell or share your personal information.

Data Retention

Only the minimum information required for synchronization is retained. Information may be deleted upon request.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting our support team.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be published on this page.
